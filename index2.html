Restaurant Description

The Math Gawdess Sushi Restaurant was inspired by Mathematician Dalanda Adams from her love and passion for Japenese cuisine. Having traveled the world Dalanda has absorbed and embraced the culture to bring this fine dining experience to the Pythagorean city of Geometry. She has searched near and far to produce a team of Mathematicians and Chefs that embody what the Math Gawdess restaurant has become today.
